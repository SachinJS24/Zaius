WHAT IS ZAIUS?

Zaius is B2C behavioral analytics CRM that that gathers customer data from channels into single system of record, applies lifecycle models, and shows you how your customers behaving - in real time.

Using Zaius, you can view any segment of customers (loyal, repeat purchases, at-risk etc), and can immediately automate realtime campaigns across email, web, mobile and other channels.
